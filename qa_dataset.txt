Q1: Why can neural networks approximate complex functions better than linear models?
A: Neural networks use multiple layers and non-linear activation functions to model complex patterns.

Q2: What limitation of RNNs do transformers overcome?
A: Transformers overcome long-range dependency issues by using self-attention instead of sequential processing.

Q3: Why is self-attention important in transformers?
A: It allows each word to consider all other words in the sequence for better context.

Q4: What is the real-world impact of overfitting?
A: Overfitting causes models to fail on unseen data despite performing well on training data.

Q5: How does depth help in deep learning?
A: Deeper layers learn hierarchical and abstract representations of data.
